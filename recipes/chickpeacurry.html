Chickpea Curry

Description

A delicious chickpea curry recipe that uses easy-to-find ingredients and comes together in about half an hour, making it perfect for a quick but filling weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, roughly chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1-2 tbs Thai red curry paste
  • 2 cans low fat coconut milk
  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1-2 tbs soy sauce
  • juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, roughly chopped
