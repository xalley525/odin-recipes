Chickpea Curry

Description

A delicious chickpea curry recipe that uses easy-to-find ingredients and comes together in about half an hour, making it perfect for a quick but filling weeknight dinner. Best served over rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, roughly chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 1-2 tbs Thai red curry paste
  • 2 cans low fat coconut milk
  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1-2 tbs soy sauce
  • juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, roughly chopped

Steps

  1. Chop onions, garlic, and cilantro and juice the lime
  2. Melt butter in a large, deep skillet over medium-low heat
  3. Add onions and saute until slightly translucent
  4. Add garlic and cook for another 1 minute
  5. Add 1 tbs curry paste and stir until onions and garlic are evenly coated
  6. Add coconut milk and stir until curry paste is disolved
  7. Taste test! If curry flavor isn't strong enough, add more!
  8. When the flavor is where you want it, add in drained and rinsed chickpeas
  9. Increase heat to medium and bring curry to a slow boil
  10. Once curry is boiling reduce heat add in lime juice, soy sayce, and cilantro
  11. Simmer gently for 2-3 minutes, tasting and adjusting lime or cilantro as needed
